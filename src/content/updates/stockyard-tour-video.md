This clip is a quick look at **outbound-ready pipe stock** at Navkar Tubes & Tools — how bundles sit in the yard, how loading areas are used, and why buyers prefer seeing real handling before placing a large order.

## What the walkthrough is meant to show

- Scale of staged MS pipe inventory
- Practical access for loading vehicles
- Everyday working conditions at an Ahmedabad stockyard
- Why photos and video beat brochure claims for industrial buyers

It is not a substitute for a size confirmation. Use it to understand our operation, then send the exact requirement to sales.

## Visiting in person

Serious project buyers are welcome to schedule a **short yard visit**. Call ahead so we can walk you to the relevant stacks and discuss documents, timings and pickup rules. Unannounced visits are harder to support during peak loading.

## After you watch

Send product name, size and quantity via WhatsApp or the [quote form](/contact/). For standard ERW and hollow-section lines we aim for a **same-day response** on availability and pricing. Video context plus a clear BOM is the fastest path from enquiry to loading.
