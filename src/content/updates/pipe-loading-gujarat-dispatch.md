Once your order is confirmed, pipes do not sit in a spreadsheet — they are **physically staged, checked and loaded** from our Vatva or Rakhial locations. This update shows outbound handling for Gujarat project sites and regular industrial buyers who need predictable dispatch.

## How dispatch usually works

1. Sales locks sizes, quantities and documents against available stock
2. Yard team stages bundles by size for the load
3. Loading is scheduled around vehicle type and site access
4. Delivery or buyer pickup is coordinated with your site contact

For large BOMs we may split loads across days or yards. You are told in advance if a line ships from Rakhial instead of Vatva.

## What to share for smooth logistics

Include with every dispatch-ready order:

- Full delivery address and pin code
- Site contact name and phone
- Preferred vehicle notes (trailer access, timing windows)
- Any unloading constraints
- Invoice / GST details already agreed at quote stage

Missing site contacts are the most common cause of delayed trucks. One WhatsApp message with the five points above keeps loading on track.

## Coverage across Gujarat

We regularly move material toward Ahmedabad metro sites and industrial belts across the state. For destinations outside our usual lanes, sales confirms transport options and timing before you commit.
