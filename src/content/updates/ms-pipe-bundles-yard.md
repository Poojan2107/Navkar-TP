Yard photography helps buyers see **how material is actually held** — bundled, tagged and accessible — before they commit. This snapshot from Navkar Tubes shows MS pipe bundles prepared for confirmation against customer bills of materials.

## Reading a yard photo the right way

A photo proves stock exists on that day; it does not replace a size check. Always treat images as context and send your BOM for a formal availability reply. That protects both sides when a popular NB moves between morning and afternoon.

## What “yard ready” means for you

- Bundles are identifiable by size grouping
- Loading paths are clear for outbound vehicles
- Sales can match your list to staged material quickly
- Mixed lines (ERW + fittings) can be planned in one pickup

If your project needs material held against a purchase order, tell sales early. Short holds are possible when stock and commercial terms allow.
