**GI square and rectangular hollow sections** are a core line for Navkar Tubes as an authorized Jindal channel partner. Fabricators use them for frames, mezzanines, roofing structures, machine guards and architectural steelwork where corrosion resistance and clean edges matter.

## What this yard snapshot shows

Bundles of GI hollow sections are tagged and stacked for Gujarat dispatch. Stock moves with project demand, so the photo is a **current yard view**, not a permanent inventory list. Always confirm the size matrix with sales before locking a fabrication schedule.

## Size planning tips for fabricators

When you prepare a hollow-section enquiry, list:

- Side × side (or width × height for rectangular)
- Wall thickness
- Length per piece and total quantity
- Whether you need cut-to-length or standard lengths
- Any preference for Jindal GI versus other stocked mill lines

Incomplete matrices slow quotes. A clean spreadsheet or WhatsApp list with those five fields usually gets a same-day response.

## Pairing hollow sections with pipes and fittings

Jobs that mix **hollow sections for frames and ERW for services** are common. Navkar can quote both from Ahmedabad stock and add mild steel fittings where the drawing calls for them. That keeps delivery windows aligned and reduces partial shipments.
