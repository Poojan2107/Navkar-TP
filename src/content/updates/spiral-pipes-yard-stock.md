**Spiral welded pipes** fill a different role from standard ERW — larger diameters, longer runs and heavier civil or utility work. Navkar Tubes stocks spiral pipe for water transmission, drainage, industrial utilities and project packages where ERW alone is not enough.

## Where spiral pipe is typically used

Buyers in Gujarat specify spiral for:

- Water supply and irrigation trunk lines
- Drainage and storm-water schemes
- Industrial utility headers
- Large-diameter structural or casing applications (confirm grade with sales)

Because diameters and wall schedules vary widely, we treat every spiral enquiry as a **size-and-quantity match** against current yard stock rather than a catalogue promise.

## What we need from your enquiry

To confirm availability and transport from Ahmedabad, share:

- Outside diameter and wall thickness
- Required length and total meters or pieces
- Coating or end finish if specified
- Site address or preferred pickup point
- Target delivery window

Our team checks staged bundles, notes any mill or test documents available for that lot, and returns a clear quotation. If stock is short on a particular OD, we tell you straight and suggest the nearest workable alternate where it makes sense.

## Bundling with ERW and fittings

Many projects need **spiral for the main run and ERW or fittings for branches**. One Navkar enquiry can cover both — plus flanges where required — so procurement does not chase three vendors for one bill of materials.
